Web Design

Web design is the creation of websites and pages to reflect a company’s brand and information and ensure a user-friendly experience. Appearance and design are incorporated as vital elements whether you’re designing a website, mobile app or maintaining content on a web page. Gaining web design skills can help you in applying for roles where your creativity could help a business improve their brand, their message and their bottom line. In this article, we take a look at web design, including what web designers do and the common web-designing elements they work with and use.
